## Context

Harbor Fit Studio runs small-group strength and mobility classes in a neighborhood where national chains, boutique studios, and fitness apps all compete for attention. Referrals were strong, but paid marketing had no consistent voice or measurement rhythm.

## Challenge

Previous vendors delivered attractive assets, but the ads felt disconnected from the studio's premium positioning. Local targeting was broad, trial offers leaned too heavily on discounts, and the owner could not tell which channel actually drove new bookings.

## What MaaxGen delivered

MaaxGen tightened the message around coached, small-group training for busy professionals who wanted structure without a big-box gym experience. We rebuilt the campaign around geo-focused search, social creative, and a simple ad-to-schedule path.

The bundle also included landing page copy, refreshed social creative, and a monthly report that tied spend to trial signups, class fill patterns, and next tests.

## Results

Trial signups became more predictable, and the studio protected premium pricing instead of racing on discounts. The owner had clearer language for referrals, ads, and landing pages, so local prospects heard the same promise at every step.
